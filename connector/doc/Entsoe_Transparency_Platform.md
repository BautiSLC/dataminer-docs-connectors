# Entsoe Transparency Platform

## About

DataMiner integrates with the ENTSO-E Transparency Platform to retrieve 15-minute day-ahead electricity market prices for a selected bidding zone or area, enabling streamlined access to forecast pricing data within your operational workflows.

## Key Benefits

- **Cost-efficient operation decisions**: By bringing ENTSO-E day-ahead pricing into DataMiner, operators gain real-time visibility into upcoming energy cost fluctuations, enabling smarter planning, optimized consumption, and more cost-efficient operational decisions.

## Prerequisites

- **API key**: The connector requires an API key. You can obtain this key by registering on the [Entsoe Transparency Platform](https://transparency.entsoe.eu/).
