# Entsoe Transparency Platform

## About

DataMiner integrates with the ENTSO-E Transparency Platform to retrieve 15-minute day-ahead electricity market prices for a selected bidding zone or area, enabling streamlined access to forecast pricing data within your operational workflows.


## Key Benefits

- **Cost-Efficient operation decission**: By bringing ENTSO-E day-ahead pricing into DataMiner, operators gain real-time visibility into upcoming energy cost fluctuations, enabling smarter planning, optimized consumption, and more cost-efficient operational decisions.

## Technical Reference

### Prerequisites

- **API Key**: The connector requires an API key. The key can be obtained by registering on [Entsoe Transparancy Platform](https://transparency.entsoe.eu/)
